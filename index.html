About Me

Hello there! My name is Kayla Wilson. I am currently working towards my masters in computer science at New Mexico State University. In December of 2023, I graduated with my Bachelors in computer science from New Mexico State University as well. In the summer of 2023, I had the privledge of interning with FAST Enterprises in Lincoln, Nebraska as an Implementation Consultant. This was my first expereince living away from my hometown in New Mexico and I loved it!

While getting in my undergraduate, I participated in a program called Google Tech Exchange where I was able to learn about programming concepts and professionalism from Google Software Engineers. I went on to be a mentor for the Google Tech Exchange program as well. Since starting my undergraduate I have also worked for Young Women in Computing, focusing on outreach for K-12 education to bring more awareness and minorities into the field of computer science. Through YWiC I also offer tutoring services for other students in computer science at my college and work towards creating a supporite community within our department.

Aside from my professional work, I enjoy playing volleyball, painting, and watching anime. Some of my favorite shows have been Blue Eye Samauri, Banana Fish, My Happy Marriage, Erased, and many others. I have recently been very fond of skincare and having a routine, as it is a form of self-care for myself.
